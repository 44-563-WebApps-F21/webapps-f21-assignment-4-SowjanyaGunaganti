LORD GORING
.

Love-letter?

  MRS. CHEVELEY
.

[_Laughing_.]

‘I want you. I trust you. I am coming to you. Gertrude.’

[LORD GORING _rushes to the bureau and takes up the envelope_, _finds is empty_, _and turns round_.]

  LORD GORING
.

wretched woman, must you always be thieving? Give me back that letter. I’ll take it from you by force. You shall not leave my room till I have got it.

[rushes towards her_, _but_ MRS. CHEVELEY _at once puts her hand on the electric bell that is on the table_. _The bell sounds with shrill reverberations_, _and_ PHIPPS _enters_.]

  MRS. CHEVELEY

[a pause_.]

Lord Goring merely rang that you should show me out. Good-night, Lord Goring!t

[out followed by_ PHIPPS. _Her face is illumined with evil triumph_. _There is joy in her eyes_. _Youth seems to have come back to her_. _Her last glance is like a swift arrow_. LORD GORING _bites his lip_, _and lights a cigarette_.]
